Essential Materials

Wax

Soy wax, paraffin wax, and beeswax are common types used in candle making.

Wicks

Choose cotton or wooden wicks depending on candle style.

Fragrance Oils

Add scents like lavender, vanilla, or rose to attract customers.

Dyes & Colors

Create visually appealing candles using safe color dyes.

Required Equipment

Melting Equipment

Double boiler or wax melter to melt wax safely.

Thermometer

Ensure proper temperature for mixing fragrance and pouring.

Molds & Containers

Glass jars, tins, or molds for shaping candles.

Pouring Jug

Used to pour melted wax safely into molds.

Stirring Tools

Wooden or metal tools to mix wax and fragrance evenly.
